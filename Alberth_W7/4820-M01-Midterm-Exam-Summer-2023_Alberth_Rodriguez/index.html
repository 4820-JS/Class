GALAXY 'ZA!

Pizza delivered to your door, anywhere in the universe!

================ PART 1 ================ GALAXY 'ZA delivers to 4 planets: Saturn, Jupiter, Mars and Venus. Names are spelt a bit differently on different planets. Saturn has 'aa' instead of just 'a'; Jupiter only has 'oo' vowels, instead of 'a', 'e' and 'i'; Mars has no vowels and Venus has 'u' for 'a', 'e', and 'i'.

Example:

Saturn - Trista Townsend ===> Tristaa Townsend
Jupiter - Trista Townsend ===> Troostoo Townsoond
Mars - Trista Townsend ===> Trst Twnsnd
Venus - Trista Townsend ===> Trustu Tuwnsund

Your task is to display the name that is typed into the input box to the output area in the webpage below, translated to the planet they have inputted. Make sure your output message is clear and easy to understand.
